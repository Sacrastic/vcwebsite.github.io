Tapan Kumar Shandilya
Vice-Chancellor
Dr.Shyama Prasad Mukherjee University

Tapan Kumar Shandilya, who is presently principal, College of Commerce, Arts and Science, Patna has been appointed as the Vice Chancellor of the Dr Shyama Prasad Mukherjee University. The Dr Shyama Prasad Mukherjee University which is state 's smallest varsity has been carved out of Ranchi University. Ranchi College on April 11, 2017 was upgraded as Unitary University, named as Dr. Shyama Prasad Mukherjee University, Ranchi.
